Our philosophy

Garden is an ideal place, where you can hide from the modern society full of technology and stay in comfortable conditions at the same time. As everybody has different tastes in art, music, food and free-time activities so a vision of perfect garden is always different.

Working with clients I try to understand their feelings and needs and to mirror them in our projects. We’re experienced in working with different landscape design styles from classical to minimalistic, Japanese to еclecticism and can create modern and convenient garden regardless of the style you prefer. We think critically about the context we are working in, by envisioning the highest use of a place, and by realizing that a successful landscape is as poetic as it is practical.

Your taste, your feelings, your life. Our experience and professionalism.

Landscape design includes 4 stages: Pre-design, Conceptual design, Design development, Construction documents.

Pre-design

We develop and refine a “vision” for the development’s exterior. The expectation of wants and needs of those who will live, work, study, or play in the space are also explored.

The stage is also includes learning topography, solar access, wind and weather patterns, water, vegetation, historic precedents, and ecology define rough budgets and timelines.

Conceptual design

We present concepts that meet the goals of your shared vision. The value of different amenity features such as bbq areas, lounge seating/gathering spaces, overhead shade structures, fire elements, pools/hot tubs, sculptural water features, and site walls and screens are evaluated and considered. A preliminary analysis of the budget can be made at this stage.

Design development

We will create a series of detail drawings, each one further fleshing out the basic concepts for the space with structural notations for patios, shade structures and canopies, rooftop pools/hot tubs, and notations for amenities that enhance and reinforce the theme you’ve chosen.

Construction documents

A typical Construction Document package includes dimensioned layout plans, site and hardscape layout plans, site amenity layout plans, low voltage landscape lighting layout plans, planting plans, irrigation plans, and construction details for all amenity features in your project.
